District: Paschim Bardhaman

About Paschim Bardhaman

Paschim Bardhaman is significant culturally due to its industrial heritage and contributions to the socio-economic development of West Bengal. The district has a mix of urban and rural cultures, with an emphasis on both traditional and modern art forms. The region's historical sites, such as the Curzon Gate, reflect its colonial past. Paschim Bardhaman also celebrates traditional festivals like Durga Puja and Saraswati Puja with grandeur.
