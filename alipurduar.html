About Alipurduar

Alipurduar is a district located in the state of West Bengal, India. The region is known for its rich cultural heritage, which is a blend of various ethnic groups including indigenous tribes and Bengali communities. The district is famous for its traditional folk music, dance forms, and festivals that showcase the diversity of its population.
