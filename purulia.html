About Purulia

Nadia, the capital of West Bengal, is a major cultural hub and has played a significant role in the history of India's independence movement. It's known for its literature, art, music, and film industry (Tollywood). The city was home to many renowned figures such as Rabindranath Tagore, Satyajit Ray, and Raja Rammohan Roy.
