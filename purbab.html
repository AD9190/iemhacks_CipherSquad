District: Purba Bardhaman

About Purba Bardhaman

Purba Bardhaman, similar to its western counterpart, is culturally relevant for its historical landmarks and celebrations of traditional festivals. The district hosts various cultural events, art exhibitions, and performances that showcase the local talent and heritage. Festivals like Kali Puja and Lakshmi Puja are celebrated with enthusiasm, featuring beautifully crafted idols and cultural programs.
